═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                      📊 HOW IT WORKS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

STEP 1: DATA COLLECTION (Daily - 2 minutes)
   Parent logs: mood (1-5), focus (1-5), social (1-5), tantrums (count), 
                sleep (hours), notes (optional)

STEP 2: FEATURE EXTRACTION (Automatic)
   System analyzes last 30 days:
   • Calculates 20+ behavioral features
   • Measures patterns and consistency
   • Identifies outliers and trends
   • Counts concerning indicators

STEP 3: PROFILE CLASSIFICATION (Automatic)
   Maps to 6 psyche clusters:
   • Rule-based classification logic
   • Multi-threshold evaluation
   • Confidence score calculation
   • Risk level determination

STEP 4: DIMENSION SCORING (Automatic)
   Scores 6 behavioral dimensions:
   • Emotional Stability (0-100)
   • Impulse Control (0-100)
   • Social Engagement (0-100)
   • Self-Regulation (0-100)
   • Cognitive Focus (0-100)
   • Emotional Resilience (0-100)

STEP 5: RECOMMENDATION GENERATION (Automatic)
   Creates personalized plan:
   • Immediate crisis interventions (if needed)
   • Weekly actionable strategies
   • Long-term development goals
   • Matched parenting approach
   • Success tracking indicators
   • Professional resource recommendations

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. LOG BEHAVIOR (Existing, Enhanced)

   GET /behavior/<child_id>/behavior-patterns
   Returns: Detailed metrics across emotional, impulse, sleep, social dimensions

   GET /behavior/<child_id>/risk-report
   Returns: Risk level, score, concerns, professional evaluation recommendations

6. GET INSIGHTS (Existing, Now Enhanced)
   GET /behavior/<child_id>/insights
   Returns: Quick overview with emoji summary

                      🎓 PSYCHE PROFILES AT A GLANCE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

✅ BALANCED & RESILIENT
   Risk: LOW
   Traits: Stable, good focus, social, self-regulated
   Action: Maintain and develop further

⚡ HIGH ENERGY / IMPULSIVE
   Risk: MEDIUM
   Traits: High activity, quick reactions, needs structure
   Actions: Consistent routines, physical activity, clear rules, timed transitions

😰 SENSITIVE / ANXIOUS
   Risk: MEDIUM
   Traits: High sensitivity, worries, needs reassurance
   Actions: Predictable routines, breathing exercises, gradual exposure, reassurance

😔 WITHDRAWN / RESERVED
   Risk: HIGH
   Traits: Low social, quiet, isolated
   Actions: Gentle encouragement, find interests, low-pressure opportunities, monitor mood

🚨 CHALLENGING / HIGH-RISK
   Risk: HIGH
   Traits: Tantrums, aggression, poor sleep, volatile
   Actions: PROFESSIONAL EVALUATION URGENT, calm-down space, clear consequences

😞 LOW ENERGY / LETHARGIC
   Risk: HIGH
   Traits: Low mood, lacks motivation, lethargic
   Actions: MEDICAL CHECK-UP, light exposure, gentle activity, professional support
