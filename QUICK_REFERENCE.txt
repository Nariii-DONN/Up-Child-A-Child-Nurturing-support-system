 🧠 THE 6 PSYCHE PROFILES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

✅ BALANCED & RESILIENT
   Emoji: ✅ | Risk: LOW
   → Emotionally stable, good impulse control, positive social engagement
   ⚡ Action: Maintain and develop further
   
⚡ HIGH ENERGY / IMPULSIVE  
   Emoji: ⚡ | Risk: MEDIUM
   → High activity, quick reactions, needs structure and guidance
   ⚡ Actions:
      • Establish consistent routines (wake/sleep)
      • 20+ mins vigorous physical activity daily
      • Use timers for transitions (5 min warnings)
      • Set ONE clear daily rule
      
😰 SENSITIVE / ANXIOUS
   Emoji: 😰 | Risk: MEDIUM
   → High sensitivity, worries frequently, needs reassurance
   ⚡ Actions:
      • Create predictable routine with visual schedule
      • Teach breathing: 4-count in, 6-count out
      • Use identical bedtime every night
      • Identify specific worry triggers
      
😔 WITHDRAWN / RESERVED
   Emoji: 😔 | Risk: HIGH ⚠️
   → Low social engagement, quiet, needs gentle encouragement
   ⚡ Actions:
      • Never force social interaction
      • Find one trusted peer or activity
      • Create low-pressure opportunities
      • Monitor for depression signals
      
🚨 CHALLENGING / HIGH-RISK
   Emoji: 🚨 | Risk: HIGH ⚠️
   → Tantrums, aggression, poor sleep, volatile behavior
   ⚡ Actions:
      • SCHEDULE PEDIATRICIAN APPOINTMENT
      • Create calm-down space
      • Clear rules with immediate consequences
      • PROFESSIONAL EVALUATION URGENT
      
😞 LOW ENERGY / LETHARGIC
   Emoji: 😞 | Risk: HIGH ⚠️
   → Low mood, lacks motivation, poor sleep/energy
   ⚡ Actions:
      • MEDICAL CHECK-UP (thyroid, vitamins)
      • 30 mins morning sunlight daily
      • One enjoyable activity together
      • Possible depression—seek professional help

 📊 6 BEHAVIORAL DIMENSIONS (Scored 0-100)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

🎭 EMOTIONAL STABILITY          ← Mood consistency & regulation
   HIGH (70+) = Stable moods    MEDIUM (40-70) = Some ups/downs    LOW (<40) = Very volatile

⚡ IMPULSE CONTROL               ← Managing reactions & frustration
   GOOD (70+) = Rarely tantrums MODERATE (40-70) = Occasional      POOR (<40) = Frequent outbursts

🤝 SOCIAL ENGAGEMENT           ← Peer interaction & group participation
   HIGH (70+) = Loves interaction MODERATE (40-70) = Balanced      LOW (<40) = Prefers alone time

🛏️ SELF-REGULATION              ← Sleep, focus, routine adherence
   STRONG (70+) = Good habits    DEVELOPING (40-70) = Some issues  WEAK (<40) = Poor routines

🎯 COGNITIVE FOCUS              ← Attention span & task completion
   STRONG (70+) = Can concentrate MODERATE (40-70) = Distractible   WEAK (<40) = Hard to focus

💪 EMOTIONAL RESILIENCE         ← Bounce-back ability after difficulties
   HIGH (70+) = Bounces back quickly MODERATE (40-70) = Some recovery LOW (<40) = Struggles to recover

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
 🚨 RED FLAG SCORING SYSTEM
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

RED FLAG INDICATORS:
   • Tantrums > 2 per day           = 2 flags
   • Sleep < 7 hours on 40%+ days   = 1 flag  
   • Average mood < 2.5/5            = 2 flags
   • Social engagement < 2/5         = 1 flag
   • Mood swings > 1.5 points/day   = 1 flag
   • Focus ability < 2/5             = 1 flag

SCORE INTERPRETATION:
   ✅ 0 flags     = LOW RISK        (Green light - normal development)
   🟡 1-2 flags   = MONITOR         (Yellow light - watch carefully)
   ⚠️  3-4 flags   = MEDIUM-HIGH     (Orange light - intervention recommended)
   🚨 4+ flags    = HIGH RISK       (Red light - PROFESSIONAL EVAL NEEDED)

 📱 NEW API ENDPOINTS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

LOG BEHAVIOR
   POST /behavior/<child_id>/log
   Headers: Authorization: Bearer <token>
   Body: { mood: 1-5, focus: 1-5, social: 1-5, tantrums: 0-5, sleep_hours: 8 }
   → Automatic AI analysis triggered

GET PSYCHE PROFILE ⭐
   GET /behavior/<child_id>/psyche-profile
   → Full profile with all 6 dimensions + risk assessment

GET RECOMMENDATIONS ⭐
   GET /behavior/<child_id>/recommendations
   → Immediate actions + weekly strategies + long-term goals

GET BEHAVIOR PATTERNS ⭐
   GET /behavior/<child_id>/behavior-patterns
   → Detailed metrics: emotional, impulse, sleep, social, trends

GET RISK REPORT ⭐
   GET /behavior/<child_id>/risk-report
   → Risk level, concerns, urgent actions, professional resources

GET INSIGHTS (ENHANCED)
   GET /behavior/<child_id>/insights
   → Quick overview with emoji-based summary

 🚨 CRISIS INDICATORS - WHEN TO SEEK IMMEDIATE HELP
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

🚨 CALL 988 (CRISIS LIFELINE) or 911 IF:
   • Child expresses suicidal thoughts
   • Self-harm behavior observed
   • Harm to others or extreme aggression
   • Severe panic or dissociation
   • Substance abuse concerns
   • Abuse or neglect suspected

📞 SCHEDULE PROFESSIONAL EVALUATION IF:
   • 4+ red flags present
   • Declining behavior despite interventions
   • Chronic anxiety or depression
   • Sleep problems lasting weeks
   • Bullying or social isolation
   • Developmental delays noticed

 📋 DAILY LOGGING GUIDE (2 MINUTES)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

MOOD (1-5 Scale):
   😢 = 1 (Very sad/upset)
   😞 = 2 (Sad)
   😐 = 3 (OK/Neutral)
   😊 = 4 (Good/Happy)
   😄 = 5 (Very happy/excited)

FOCUS (1-5 Scale):
   1 = Can't concentrate at all
   2 = Easily distracted
   3 = OK, some attention
   4 = Good focus
   5 = Excellent concentration

SOCIAL (1-5 Scale):
   1 = Isolating/withdrawn
   2 = Prefers alone time
   3 = Balanced social time
   4 = Good peer interaction
   5 = Loves being with others

TANTRUMS (Count):
   0 = No tantrums/outbursts
   1 = One minor episode
   2 = Couple episodes
   3+ = Multiple or severe

SLEEP (Hours):
   Ideal: 8-10 hours for most kids
   Note any: too much/too little/poor quality

OPTIONAL NOTES:
   • What triggered mood/behavior?
   • Any positive moments?
   • Changes noticed?
