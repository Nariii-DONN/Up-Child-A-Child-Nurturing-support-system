🧠 THE 6 PSYCHE PROFILES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  ✅  BALANCED & RESILIENT
      Emoji: ✅ | Risk: LOW
      → Emotionally stable, good focus, positive social
      Key Action: Maintain and develop further

  ⚡  HIGH ENERGY / IMPULSIVE
      Emoji: ⚡ | Risk: MEDIUM
      → High activity, quick reactions, needs structure
      Key Actions: Routines, physical activity, clear rules, timers

  😰  SENSITIVE / ANXIOUS
      Emoji: 😰 | Risk: MEDIUM
      → High sensitivity, worries, needs reassurance
      Key Actions: Predictability, breathing exercises, gradual exposure

  😔  WITHDRAWN / RESERVED
      Emoji: 😔 | Risk: HIGH
      → Low social, quiet, isolated
      Key Actions: Gentle encouragement, find interests, low-pressure

  🚨  CHALLENGING / HIGH-RISK
      Emoji: 🚨 | Risk: HIGH
      → Tantrums, aggression, poor sleep
      Key Action: PROFESSIONAL EVALUATION URGENT

  😞  LOW ENERGY / LETHARGIC
      Emoji: 😞 | Risk: HIGH
      → Low mood, lacks motivation, lethargic
      Key Action: MEDICAL CHECK-UP, light exposure, support

🚨 RISK ASSESSMENT SYSTEM
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

RED FLAGS IDENTIFIED:
  • High tantrums (> 2/day)
  • Sleep deprivation (< 7 hrs on 40%+ days)
  • Persistent low mood (< 2.5/5 average)
  • Social withdrawal (< 2/5 engagement)
  • Mood volatility (> 1.5 point swings)
  • Poor focus (< 2/5)

RISK LEVEL MATRIX:
  ✅ 0 flags     = LOW RISK        (Green light)
  🟡 1-2 flags   = MONITOR         (Yellow light)
  ⚠️  3-4 flags   = MEDIUM-HIGH     (Orange light)
  🚨 4+ flags    = HIGH RISK       (Red light)

ACTIONABLE ALERTS:
  • Automatic concern identification
  • Professional evaluation recommendations
  • Crisis alert system
  • Trend-based predictions


📱 NEW API ENDPOINTS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

  POST /behavior/<child_id>/log
      → Log daily behavior entry (triggers auto-analysis)

  ⭐ GET /behavior/<child_id>/psyche-profile
      → Full profile with all dimensions & risk assessment

  ⭐ GET /behavior/<child_id>/recommendations
      → Immediate actions, strategies, goals, parenting style

  ⭐ GET /behavior/<child_id>/behavior-patterns
      → Detailed pattern analysis across all dimensions

  ⭐ GET /behavior/<child_id>/risk-report
      → Risk level, concerns, professional recommendations

  GET /behavior/<child_id>/insights
      → Quick overview (enhanced with new AI insights)
